## Usage Notes

1. **Primary vs Secondary:** When an onset has both, prefer primary domain usage
2. **Expansion:** Empty slots (â€”) are available for new vocabulary
3. **Existing words take precedence:** Don't fight established vocabulary
4. **Glottal forms ('):** Reserved for sacred/abstract concepts
5. **Ejectives (^):** Often carry special/emphatic meaning
6. **Affricates (Æ¨, Æ¶, Å§, Å¡):** Used for distinctive/important concepts
